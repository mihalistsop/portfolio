Performance Analysis forManchester City

General Information about the analysis

In this analysis, we delve into the performance of Manchester City in the past season to gain insights into their strengths, weaknesses
and overall team dynamics.

By examining various metrics and statistics, I aim to provide a comprehensive assessment of the team's performance using mostly Tableau and Microsoft Excel.

To conduct this analysis, we gathered a vast array of data from Manchester City's matches throughout the season, with the help of the infamous and accurate Sport Data Collecting company, Opta. This data includes Match results, Player statistics and Team formations. 

The dataset was carefully curated and organized to ensure accuracy and relevance. I utilized various sources, such as official match reports, player performance databases, and sports analytics websites, to compile a comprehensive and reliable dataset for our analysis.

We focused on key performance metrics to evaluate Manchester City's performance in both home and away matches. Specifically, we analyzed their ball possession percentages to assess their ability to control the game and convert opportunities into favorable results.

The bar charts allow viewers to easily compare the ball possession of Manchester City in different scenarios. By examining the height of the bars, viewers can assess the level of possession dominance during home and away matches. Higher bars indicate a greater percentage of ball possession, highlighting Manchester City's control over the game in each setting.

Manchester City's Assists and Shots

Analysis

In this analysis, we explore the total assists made by Manchester City players and their shot attempts both outside and inside the opponent's area. By presenting the findings in a comprehensive table format, we aim to provide a clear overview of the team's creative playmaking and shooting tendencies.

In this analysis, we focus on Manchester City's ability to regain possession of the ball successfully in the middle third of the field. By examining this specific aspect of their gameplay, we aim to understand their effectiveness in disrupting opponent's attacks and initiating their own offensive moves.

TreeMap of Successful Ball Regains

To present the findings in an insightful and visually appealing manner, we created a treemap visualization. The treemap provides a hierarchical representation of the successful ball regains, with the size of each rectangle corresponding to the frequency or quantity of regains. The treemap allows for a quick and intuitive understanding of the distribution and importance of successful regains of the middle third based on the players' position in the match.

The analysis of Manchester City's successful ball regains in the middle third sheds light on the team's defensive strength and ability to initiate their own offensive moves. The treemap visualization offers a clear and concise representation of the data, providing valuable insights into the areas of the middle third where Manchester City displayed exceptional performance. Understanding these patterns can inform future tactical decisions and further enhance the team's overall gameplay strategy.

In this analysis, we present a visually appealing bubble chart showcasing the big chances created by Manchester City players based on their positions.

 By using a bubble chart, we aim to provide a comprehensive overview of player contributions in creating significant scoring opportunities, while also highlighting the distribution of these chances across different positions.

 The implemented bubble chart visually represents the big chances created by Manchester City players, with each bubble representing an individual player. The size of the bubble corresponds to the number of big chances created by the player, indicating their level of contribution.
